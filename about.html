Who We Are

The Heart of XOX Coatings

At XOX Coatings we provide spray painting services that transform our customers’ homes, whilst saving them money and hassle. “How is that so?” you ask. Well, if you have just had a lovely new composite front door installed, in the ever-popular Anthracite Grey, you will know it wasn’t cheap. You will also know that there was a bit of remedial work required to the walls around the new frame and likely a fresh coat of paint. You will also know that changing all of the windows in your house to match is also going to be expensive and incur the same issues.

Now imagine you can have all of your windows magically coloured in, in a much faster timescale, without any dusty building work, without any repairs needed and at less than half the cost! Well, imagine no more. At XOX Coatings we can spray paint your uPVC windows in an industrial 2K polyurethane coating (the most durable product available on the market) with an expected lifespan of 10-15 years.
